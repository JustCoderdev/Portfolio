Colorspace test

This is to demonstrate why hsl (after being a good choice for a long time) should now be deprecated in favour of Oklch (and its colorspace)

HSL - Hue Saturation Lightness

45 80% 20%
45 80% 40%
45 80% 60%

OKLCH - Lightness Chroma Hue

50% 0.15 90
70% 0.15 90
90% 0.15 90

As you can see (I hope) in the HSL version, the darker yellow loses a bit of saturation meanwhile in the OKLCH palette all colors keep the same "yellowness"

This test was performed by taking the HSL colors, converting them to OKLCH, and then modifying the lightness value to match as much as possible the original colors while keeping the same step between the darker and lighter version of the base yellow
