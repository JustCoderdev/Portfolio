About me

I am JustCode, a nostalic "citizen of the web", stubborn and dedicated to experience the old web in all of its flavors

Being a developer who loves old tech and legendary technologies I could not lower myself to writing JavaScript, Python or whatever the fuck is Dart. So I masochistically decided to use C from now (2022?) on, but not just C, no no no... C89 (aka Ansi-C)

My motives

Other than wanting to feel superior to any soydev playing with the latest JS framework, I wanted a language that could let me speak the ascii language of the bit god (i.e. my computer) to understand the black box and become the mechanic of the "under the hood"

I switched to linux as soon as I could (I use nixos btw) and started programming in C89 after a period of reflection and self rediscovery of my lost C skill
