No Bullshit Licence

My philosopy is that code should be free. Free to view, edit, share and free to make a living out of it but NOT free to exploit and get rich abusing open source and make a fool of FOSS developers

I want a licence that lets indie developers, small groups and even honest companies to utilise code and contribute to it while at the same time avoid lazy corporations to cut corners and straight up steal[1] the work of others disregarding licences just for the sake of money

What do I want

Permission to

  • Use in projects
  • Modify the source code
  • Distribute the source or a modified version
